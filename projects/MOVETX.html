MOVE Texas A&M

Project Description

Developed an internal website for the MOVE Texas A&M student organization. MOVE is a nonpartisan, nonprofit, grassroots organization building power in underrepresented youth communities through civic education, leadership development, and issue advocacy. At their Texas A&M chapter, they seek to spread voter rights awareness and increase voter registration among the Texas A&M community.

I worked alongside a team of four others to make a website that the organization could use for coordinating events and tracking member attendance and participation.
